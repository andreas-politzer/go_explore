3. Targeted KPI Framework

A collection of 5 key business metrics carefully split between geographical expansion data and B2B distributor network performance.

KPI 1: Revenue per Capita (Demographic Multiplier)

Establishes the historical income generated per 1 Million citizens in active markets (e.g., Sweden) to build a robust baseline expectation for new frontiers.

Formula: Total Country Revenue / Country Population (Millions)

KPI 2: Revenue-to-GDP Elasticity Index (Purchasing Power Alignment)

Safeguards expansion margins by mapping historical sales explicitly against national wealth index parameters, isolating premium consumer potential in countries like Norway.

Formula: Total Country Revenue / National GDP (Billions USD)

KPI 3: Average Order Value (AOV) by Retailer Segment

Directly answers whether specialty retail stores (e.g., Golf, Eyewear) process larger basket sizes compared to general sports and outdoor retailers.

Formula: Total Segment Revenue / Total Segment Order Count

KPI 4: Order Frequency Density (Operational Efficiency)

Measures the purchasing consistency across distributors, identifying low-maintenance high-volume partners versus resource-intensive micro-accounts.

Formula: Total Transaction Records / Count of Unique Active Stores

KPI 5: Digital Channel Penetration Rate

Tracks the operational automation level of incoming orders, monitoring what percentage of total business scales cleanly through automated web infrastructure.

Formula: (Web Revenue / Total Revenue All Channels) * 100
